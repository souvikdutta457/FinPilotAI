# FinPilot AI Roadmap

## Version 1.0 (MVP)

### ✅ Feature 1 – Smart Transaction Entry
Users can type:
"Spent ₹450 on Domino's"

The AI automatically detects:
- Amount
- Category
- Type (Income/Expense)
- Date

---

### ✅ Feature 2 – Automatic Excel Update

After understanding the transaction, the system updates the correct location in the Excel workbook automatically.

---

### ✅ Feature 3 – AI Financial Advisor

The AI analyses spending and provides insights such as:
- Spending habits
- Savings rate
- Budget performance
- Largest expenses

---

### ✅ Feature 4 – AI Chat

Users can ask questions like:
- How much did I spend on food?
- What was my biggest expense?
- Which month had the highest savings?

---

### ✅ Feature 5 – Monthly Financial Report

Generate a professional financial summary with charts and insights.

---

### ✅ Feature 6 – Financial Health Score

Generate a score out of 100 based on:
- Savings
- Spending
- Budget discipline
- Cash flow

---
